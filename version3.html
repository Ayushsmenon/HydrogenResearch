Multi-Cell Electrolyser

Input Power: 5V

Volume of Electrolyte: 304.2 mL

No of Plates: 14

Electrolyte: KOH

This is the most advanced model of HHO generators. This design features separate metal elecetrodes and rubber gaskets, totaling 14 plates, which form 7 distinct cells. This configuration allows for enhanced production of hydrogen and oxygen gases, resulting in more efficient and better control of the gas output. The introduction of individual gaskets and plates in this version significantly improves the generator's sealing and durability, reducing the risk of leaks and increases portability. Additionally, the optimized arrangement of the cells, coupled with improved materials, maximizes the active surface area for electrolysis, enhancing overall gas generation. This version represents our goal in HHO technology, focusing on reliability, efficiency, for various applications.
