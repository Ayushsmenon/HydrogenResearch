This is the second generation of our tubular-structured HHO generators. This model features three concentric tubes arranged with opposing polarities to enhance hydrogen production. Although the increase in production volume was relatively small, this design helped us understand key concepts related to hydrogen generation, including the impact of electrode spacing, polarity arrangements, and electrolyte composition. Through testing, we discovered that concentric tube structures allow for a more uniform electric field, improving the efficiency of the electrolysis process. Additionally, the design offered insights into optimizing gas separation and minimizing power loss. These learnings laid the foundation for further advancements in our next-generation models, driving us closer to more efficient and practical hydrogen generation solutions.
